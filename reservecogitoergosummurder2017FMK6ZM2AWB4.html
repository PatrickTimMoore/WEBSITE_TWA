Reserve Now!

1. ONLINE tickets are discounted in price: Adults $10, children and students $5.
Tickets at the door are: Adults $12, children and students students $7.

2. When buying tickets, choose the specific show first. You will need to purchase adult tickets separately from student tickets. Each category of tickets for each play will be a separate 'deposit' in your shopping cart before you total all ticket purchases and pay.

3. Payments are processed through a secure PayPal account to the Willows Academy.

4. Tickets will be held in purchaser's name at the Will Call Window at the Willows Academy and can be picked up 30 minutes before the show.

5. On-line access for each show on November 9th, 10th, and 11th will shut down by 3:00 pm the day before the show. Any unsold tickets for that show will be sold at the door 30 minutes prior to the show at the full price.

6. If you experience difficulty purchasing tickets, click here to contact the web page designer.
