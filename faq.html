FAQ

Why won't the paypal cart open?

  • Every time the cart is updated with new contents, it will open in a new tab unless the cart is already open in a new tab. Try checcking you other open tabs or windows for your shopping cart.

I keep getting stuck inside the paypal cart, help?

  • Paypal requires that the cart can only be updated in a currently open tab, hence you must visit your cart after every item addition. Simply click on the "continue shopping" tag on the cart screen to be returned to the show selection.

Can i edit items in my cart?

  • Yes! while in the Paypal cart page, you can easily edit the quanity of tickets by using the "+" and "-" buttons. From that page, cart entries can also be easily removed by clicking on the text "remove."
